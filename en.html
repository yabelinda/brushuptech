Challenges in Conventional Manufacturing

Traditional Metal Tufting

Relies on copper plates for fixation, limiting hole positions and density, making it difficult to meet high-end product demands. It is also prone to oxidation and bacterial growth in humid environments.

Insufficient Bristle Density and Pull Strength

Ultrasonic welding can compromise the structural strength of toothbrushes; bristles using PTT technology also tend to fall out easily.

Highly Dependent on Manual Labor

High labor costs; quality and production capacity are difficult to stabilize.

Excessive Mold Changeover Time

Long downtime makes it hard to handle high-mix, low-volume orders.
